About Us

For more than 60 years, AISSMS IOIT Cafe has been delighting its patrons with exceptional cuisine. Our cafe is a favorite spot for both students and college staff, offering a comforting cup of hot tea and bun maska. Whether you're here to savor the food or enjoy the vibrant atmosphere, we provide a welcoming space complete with a work area and free WiFi.
